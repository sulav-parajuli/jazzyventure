About Us

At Jazzy Venture, we believe that style and functionality go hand-in-hand. Founded with a vision to enhance your everyday look, we are dedicated to offering premium eyewear, stylish watches, chic purses and perfumes that not only complement your style but also elevate your confidence.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, fashionable accessories that help you express your unique personality. We understand that every accessory you choose is a reflection of who you are, and we strive to ensure that our products meet the highest standards of quality and design.

Our Products

Premium Eye Glasses

Discover a wide range of eyeglasses that combine comfort, durability, and style. Our collection features iconic brands such as Gucci, Prada, Lacoste, Oakley, Hugo Boss, Dior, Police, Dolce & Gabbana, Porsche, Carrera, Cartier, Ray-Ban, Ferrari, Montblanc, Tom Hardy, Fendi, and Vogue. Whether you prefer classic frames or trendy designs, our eyeglasses are crafted to suit every face shape and personality, ensuring you look your best while seeing the world clearly.

Watches

Our watches are more than just timepieces; they are a statement of elegance and sophistication. Explore our collection from renowned brands like Rolex, Omega, Tag Heuer, Breitling, Seiko, Patek Philippe, and Audemars Piguet. Each watch is designed to match your style and needs, offering the perfect blend of precision, luxury, and innovation.

Purses

Complete your look with our selection of purses that are both functional and fashionable. We offer meticulously designed purses from brands such as Louis Vuitton, Chanel, Hermes, Michael Kors, Coach, Tory Burch, and Kate Spade. Each purse is crafted with attention to detail, making it the perfect accessory for any occasion, whether it's a casual outing or a formal event.

Perfumes

Indulge in our exquisite collection of perfumes that capture the essence of luxury and sophistication. Featuring scents from top brands such as Chanel, Dior, Gucci, Tom Ford, Prada, and Versace, our perfumes are crafted to leave a lasting impression. Whether you prefer a fresh, floral fragrance or a bold, spicy scent, our curated selection ensures you find the perfect perfume to complement your style and mood.

Our Commitment

At Jazzy Venture Pvt. Ltd, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We are committed to providing an exceptional experience, from the moment you visit our store at "CG Landmark" to the moment you receive your purchase. We are always with you to assist you, ensuring that you find the perfect accessory to elevate your style.
Thank you for choosing Jazzy Venture Pvt. Ltd. We look forward to helping you look and feel your best with our premium accessories.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Check out our FAQs to learn more about our products and services.

What products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of eyeglasses, watches, purses, and perfumes. Our products include items from top brands like Gucci, Prada, Rolex, Louis Vuitton, and many more.

Where are you located?

We are located at CG Landmark, Bharatpur-10, Chitwan, Nepal (in front of Birendra Multiple Campus). You can visit our physical store on the second floor of CG Landmark.

What brands do you carry?

We carry a variety of premium brands, including Gucci, Prada, Lacoste, Oakley, Hugo Boss, Dior, Police, Dolce & Gabbana, Porsche, Carrera, Cartier, Ray-Ban, Ferrari, Montblanc, Tom Hardy, Fendi, Vogue, Rolex, Omega, Tag Heuer, Breitling, Seiko, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, Louis Vuitton, Chanel, Hermes, Michael Kors, Coach, Tory Burch, Kate Spade, and Tumi.

Are your products authentic?

Yes, all our products are 100% authentic and sourced directly from the brands or authorized distributors. We guarantee the authenticity of every item we sell.

What is your return policy?

We have a customer-friendly return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including esewa, Khalti, cheques, and other secure online payment options relied on nepal. You must required to ask before making payment. We won't be responsible for any fraud.
